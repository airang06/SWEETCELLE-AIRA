ABOUT US

Meow Cuddle Paradise is a non-profit organization dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation and rehoming of abused and neglected animals.

Meow Cuddle Paradise simply with a lot of hope, courage and the unwavering support of a few good friends. Our vision is a world where humans treat cats with respect, emphaty, and understanding.

Meow Cuddle Paradise has been helping cats and their owners since 2024, evolving and adapting to changing social issues. These include converting the shelter into open rooms, not cages, for our cats to socialise in and developing our volunteering scheme to better support those with disabilities and special needs. Times change, but our love of cats remains consistent.

Our Vision

The Meow Cuddle Paradise objectives are to relieve and prevent the suffering and distress amongst cats in need of care and attention and to promote the welfare of cats, as well as finding new homes for cats and kittens in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is defined simply as:

‘We aim to relieve and prevent suffering amongst cats.’

In collaboration with staff and volunteers across the charity, we also defined our values:

‘Through enthusiasm and collaboration, we demonstrate:

Compassion:

We offer kindness and empathy to all cats and humans.
Integrity:

We are trustworthy and honest.
Commitment:

We are attentive and aim for excellence.’

You Can Help

DONATE

As a non-profit organization, Meow Cuddle Paradise operates and relies heavily on donations from the community. We are always called upon to rescue cats suffering from abandonment, abuse, injury or neglect and non of it is possible without your support.

ADOPT

Many of our Adoptables from other shelters have been rescued from death row, from local pounds or from abusive environments. By choosing to adopt, you are giving them the chance to fiend a new home and to feel the love and security they deserve.

SPONSOR

While not everyone can foster or adopt, there are always other ways to help our rescues. Through our Sponsor-A-Rescue Program, our rescues now have a wonderful support system rooting for their full recovery and continuous comfort while at the shelter.
